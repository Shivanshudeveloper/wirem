About

Wire M is one stop solution for all your needs like electrical equipment’s, wiring, CCTV related works. On the other hand, WireM delivers services at your doorstep with maximum satisfaction and great assistance even after service. At WireM, our core service is electrical and security systems. You will be amazed to find almost every modern solution to your electrical needs. WireM is a perfect platform in which you will be provided with all the necessities of everyday life. We have been working in the market for the last four years as Secure Electricals. Now, We have come up with a rebranded vertical WireM. A new & cutting-edge system for customers to solve every problem they face in daily life. We are just a text away. You may contact us through our newly developed application, or just call or text us and we will reach to your doorstep at your given time. We are also offering great discounts for new customers and we encourage you to try our service once. Our major expertise is a nice modern electrical wiring solution for home and commercial spaces. We are also exceptional when it comes to security system solutions. We provides you with top-notch CCTV Surveillance and home automation systems. We also have exclusive tie-ups with several brands like CP-Plus, Syska, Eveready etc. That enables us to deliver many physical items and raw materials at a price you would not have even thought of. .
